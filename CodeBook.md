##Activity Labels

WALKING (value 1): During the test, the subject walked with no incline.
WALKING_UPSTAIRS (value 2): During the test, the subject walked up a staircase
WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS (value 3): During the test, the subject walked down a staircase 
SITTING (value 4): During the test, the subject was sitting 
STANDING (value 5): During the test, the subject was standing 
LAYING (value 6): During the test, the subject was laying down
